Industry Updates

Stay informed with the latest news and trends in the feeds industry.

In Central Luzon, a feed mill has been constructed to allow for greater flexibility in aquaculture diet formulation.

According to the local authority, the new mill will help reduce feed costs and support growth of the aquaculture sector in the province of Aurora.

Read More

The Philippines fish feed market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.9% during the forecast period 2025-2031.

The growth in demand for seafood, improvement in aquaculture practices and rising focus on sustainability are the major drivers of the market. Increasing awareness among consumers about high nutrition content and health benefits associated with seafood consumption is further bolstering the demand for fish feed products in this region

Government urged to address feeds, fry issues

“Due to the low quality of feeds, Filipino favorites like bangus and tilapia that are grown in fish farms now need more feeds to grow to marketable sizes,” food security advocacy group Tugon Kabuhayan convenor Asis Perez said during a bi-monthly media briefing.
