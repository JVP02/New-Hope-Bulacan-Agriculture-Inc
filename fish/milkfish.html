Species Characteristics

Understanding the Milkfish Advantage

Physical Features

  • Body: Streamlined, silvery body
  • Tail: Fork-shaped tail fin
  • Size: Can grow up to 1.7 meters
  • Weight: Average 0.5-1 kg

Habitat Requirements

  • Water: Tropical and subtropical
  • Type: Brackish water ponds
  • Area: Coastal marine waters
  • Adapt: Various salinities

Life Cycle

  • Growth: Rapid growth rate
  • Maturity: 5-7 months
  • Breeding: High reproduction
  • Lifespan: Long potential
