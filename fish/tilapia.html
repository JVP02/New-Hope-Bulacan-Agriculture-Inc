Species Characteristics

Understanding the Tilapia Advantage

Physical Features

  • Body: Deep, laterally compressed body
  • Tail: Rounded tail fin
  • Size: Can grow up to 60 cm
  • Weight: Average 1-2 kg

Habitat Requirements

  • Water: Freshwater and brackish water
  • Type: Ponds, lakes, and rivers
  • Area: Tropical and subtropical regions
  • Adapt: Wide range of salinities

Life Cycle

  • Growth: Fast growth rate
  • Maturity: 4-6 months
  • Breeding: High reproduction rate
  • Lifespan: Up to 10 years
